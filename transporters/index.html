Instructions

Match the transport mechanisms for each ion and tubular segment. You can click and drag the transporters to the correct rectangle, or select a rectangle and click on all matching transporters. Click again on a transporter inside a rectangle to remove it. Remember that in some tubular segments there are more than one cell type.

Click on the "Check" button to check your answers. You will get a second trial to correct any errors (in indicated rectangles). Click ‘‘Check’’ a second time to submit your final trial.

You get full credit for correct rectangles on the first attempt, and partial credit for the correct rectangles on the second attempt.
